<p><p>The Liberated Mineral Flotation Reagent Market encompasses various chemical agents essential for mineral processing. Flotation collectors enhance the hydrophobic properties of target minerals, enabling their separation from gangue. Flotation depressants inhibit the flotation of non-target minerals, ensuring selective recovery. Flotation activators facilitate the flotation of specific minerals by modifying surface properties. Other reagents in this market include frothers, modifiers, and dispersants, which collectively optimize the flotation process, improving efficiency and recovery rates in mineral extraction operations.</p></p>
