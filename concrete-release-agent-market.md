<p><p>The concrete release agent market is primarily divided into two types: oil-based and water-based agents. Oil-based release agents are derived from petroleum and offer excellent lubrication, preventing concrete from adhering to molds, making them ideal for intricate designs and reusable forms. In contrast, water-based release agents use biodegradable materials and are easier to clean up, making them more environmentally friendly. Both types enhance the efficiency of concrete pouring and finishing processes while ensuring smooth surface quality and easy mold release.</p></p>
