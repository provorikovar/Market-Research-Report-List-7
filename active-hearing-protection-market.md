<p><p>The Active Hearing Protection market includes two primary types: earplugs and earmuffs. Earplugs are designed to fit snugly in the ear canal, effectively blocking sound while allowing for communication and situational awareness, often using electronic components to manage noise levels. Earmuffs, on the other hand, encompass the entire ear and provide passive and active noise reduction. They often feature adjustable designs for comfort, and advanced models can amplify quieter sounds while reducing harmful noise, catering to various environments such as industrial sites and shooting ranges.</p></p>
