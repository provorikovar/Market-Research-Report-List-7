<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Fans</li><li>Axial Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial cooling fans market is primarily divided into two types: centrifugal fans and axial fans. Centrifugal fans provide high pressure and are ideal for moving air through ducts or systems with resistance, making them suitable for ventilation in large industrial spaces. On the other hand, axial fans move air parallel to the fan’s axis and are more efficient for high flow applications with low resistance, commonly used in cooling equipment and agricultural settings. Each type serves specific environmental and operational needs.</p></p>
