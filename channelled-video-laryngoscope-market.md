<p><p>The channeled video laryngoscope market is categorized into rigid and flexible fiberoptic laryngoscopes. In contrast, flexible fiberoptic laryngoscopes offer greater maneuverability and are suited for difficult airways or challenging anatomy, allowing for enhanced visualization in various clinical settings. Both types utilize advanced video technology for improved visualization during intubation procedures, ensuring higher success rates and patient safety.</p></p>
