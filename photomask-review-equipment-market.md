<p><strong>The Photomask Review Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photomask Review Equipment market features two primary methods: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method involves comparing individual die from a photomask to assess quality and alignment, ensuring each die meets specifications. In contrast, the DB method involves comparing die against a digital database of known good patterns and designs, facilitating quicker identification of defects and inconsistencies. Both methods are essential for maintaining the integrity and performance of semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
