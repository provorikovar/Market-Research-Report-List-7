<p><p>The capacitive fingerprint reader market consists of two main types: area and swipe. Area fingerprint readers capture an entire fingerprint image at once, providing high accuracy and detail, making them ideal for secure applications like mobile devices and door locks. In contrast, swipe fingerprint readers require users to slide their finger across a sensor, offering a more compact and cost-effective solution. These are commonly found in access control systems and smartphones, balancing convenience with efficient space usage.</p></p>
