<p><p>The semiconductor abatement systems market includes four main types: </p><p>1. **Combustion Type** - Utilizes high-temperature oxidation to break down harmful compounds in exhaust gases, ensuring efficient removal. </p><p>2. **Wet Type** - Employs scrubbing liquids to capture and neutralize gaseous pollutants, ideal for specific chemical processes. </p><p>3. **Dry Type** - Operates with solid adsorbents to trap contaminants without the use of liquids, suited for various gas streams. </p><p>4. **Catalytic Type** - Uses catalysts to facilitate chemical reactions at lower temperatures, enhancing efficiency in pollutant conversion. Each type caters to different industry needs and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
