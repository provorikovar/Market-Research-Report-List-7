<p><p>The floor moulding market comprises several types, each serving distinct aesthetic and functional purposes. Solid wood moulding offers natural beauty and durability, making it a popular choice. Vinyl moulding is lightweight, moisture-resistant, and often more affordable, ideal for various environments. MDF (Medium Density Fiberboard) provides a smooth surface for painting, appealing for modern designs. Plastic moulding is cost-effective and resistant to moisture, suitable for high-humidity areas.
