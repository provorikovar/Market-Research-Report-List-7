<p><p>The commercial lawn mower market features three main types: battery-powered, electric, and gas-powered mowers. Electric mowers provide consistent power and are quieter, suitable for medium-sized areas, but require access to electricity. Gas-powered mowers deliver robust performance and are effective for large, tough terrains, though they emit emissions and require more maintenance. Each type caters to different operational needs and environmental considerations in commercial landscaping.</p></p>
