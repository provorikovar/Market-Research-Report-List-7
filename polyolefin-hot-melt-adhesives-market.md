<p><p>The Polyolefin Hot Melt Adhesives market features three primary types: granular, blocky, and film. Granular adhesives come in small pellets for easy melting and application, suitable for high-speed production processes. Blocky adhesives are solid blocks that can be melted and dispensed, often offering flexibility in formulation for various applications. Film adhesives are pre-extruded sheets used in specialized bonding tasks, delivering uniform thickness and controlled adhesive distribution. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
