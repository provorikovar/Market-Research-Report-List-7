<p><strong>The Stainless Steel Globe Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flanged Connection</li><li>Threaded Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel globe valves market is primarily categorized into two main types based on connection methods: flanged connection and threaded connection. Flanged connection valves feature large, flat surfaces with bolt holes, allowing for secure, leak-proof fittings in high-pressure applications. In contrast, threaded connection valves utilize male and female threads for easy assembly and disassembly, making them suitable for smaller piping systems. Both types offer durability and resistance to corrosion, making them ideal for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
