<p><p>The Veterinary Immunodiagnostics market encompasses various testing methods for diagnosing diseases in animals. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) detects specific antibodies or antigens through enzyme-linked reactions, offering high specificity and sensitivity. Radioimmunoassay uses radiolabeled substances to quantify antigens or hormones, providing precise measurements. Rapid tests, often lateral flow assays, allow for quick results at the point of care, enhancing diagnostic efficiency. Other market methods may include immunofluorescence and western blotting, catering to diverse veterinary diagnostic needs.</p></p>
