<p><ul><li>Captive Hydrogen</li><li>Merchant Hydrogen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial hydrogen market consists of two main types: captive hydrogen and merchant hydrogen. Captive hydrogen is produced on-site for specific industrial processes, ensuring a reliable supply tailored to the needs of a facility, often linked to large-scale operations like refineries or chemical plants. In contrast, the merchant hydrogen market involves the sale of hydrogen to multiple customers from centralized production facilities. This model caters to diverse industries, providing greater flexibility and supply options without the need for individual plants.</p></p>
