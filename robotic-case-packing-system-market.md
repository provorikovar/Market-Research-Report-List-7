<p><ul><li>Side Load</li><li>Top Load</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robotic case packing system market includes two primary types: side load and top load systems. Side load systems introduce products horizontally, enabling efficient packing in cases with minimal space requirements, ideal for larger or heavier items. In contrast, top load systems operate by placing products into cases from above, offering versatility for various packaging shapes and sizes, making them suitable for lighter or irregularly shaped products. Both types enhance automation in packing processes, improving productivity and reducing labor costs.</p></p>
