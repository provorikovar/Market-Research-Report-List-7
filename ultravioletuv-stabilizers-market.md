<p><p>The Ultraviolet (UV) stabilizers market comprises several key types, including Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS), UV absorbers, and quenchers. HALS are effective in preventing degradation by scavenging free radicals. UV absorbers protect materials by absorbing harmful UV radiation and converting it into heat. Quenchers deactivate excited states of molecules to prevent damage, while "other" categories encompass various stabilizers that may offer specific functionality or complement existing technologies. Together, these stabilizers enhance the durability and performance of products exposed to UV light.</p></p>
