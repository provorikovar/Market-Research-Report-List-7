<p><p>Bipolar membranes (BPMs) are specialized ion-exchange membranes consisting of cation- and anion-exchange layers. **Homogeneous Bipolar Membranes** feature uniform, tightly bonded layers, offering high efficiency, stability, and low electrical resistance, making them ideal for precise applications like electrodialysis and water splitting. **Heterogeneous Bipolar Membranes** have less uniform layers, often with lower production costs, but higher resistance and reduced performance, suitable for less demanding applications. Both types cater to industries like energy, water treatment, and chemical processing, balancing cost and performance needs.</p></p>
