<p><p>The Alkaline Fuel Cells (AFC) market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Circulating Electrolyte Alkaline Fuel Cells** use a continuously flowing liquid electrolyte, enhancing ion transport and efficiency.</p><p>2. **Fixed Electrolyte Alkaline Fuel Cells** have a stable, solid or gel-like electrolyte that simplifies design and increases durability.</p><p>3. **Dissolved Fuel Alkaline Fuel Cells** utilize dissolved reactants in the electrolyte, promoting uniform distribution and improved reaction rates. Each type offers unique advantages suited for various applications in energy production.</p></p>
