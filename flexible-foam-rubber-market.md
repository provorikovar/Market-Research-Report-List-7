EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) is known for its excellent weather and ozone resistance, making it ideal for outdoor applications. Chloroprene (Neoprene) offers good chemical stability and flexibility, commonly used in protective gear. Other materials may include silicone and polyurethane foams, catering to specialized industrial needs.</p></p>
