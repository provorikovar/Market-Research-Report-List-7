<p><ul><li>Single Zone Vacuum Heater</li><li>Multi Zone Vacuum Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor wafer vacuum heater market comprises two main types: single zone and multi zone vacuum heaters. Single zone vacuum heaters are designed for uniform heating across a single area, making them ideal for specific applications requiring consistent temperature control. In contrast, multi zone vacuum heaters feature multiple heating zones that allow for precise temperature adjustments in different sections of the wafer. This flexibility makes multi zone heaters suitable for advanced processes with varying thermal requirements, enhancing overall efficiency and performance.</p></p>
