<p><p>Automotive CRM software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based CRM operates on the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and remote access, making it ideal for dealerships with multiple locations. Conversely, on-premise CRM is installed locally on the dealership's servers, providing tighter control over data and customizations. While cloud-based solutions often feature automatic updates and lower initial costs, on-premise options may offer enhanced security and integration with existing systems, catering to varying business needs.</p></p>
