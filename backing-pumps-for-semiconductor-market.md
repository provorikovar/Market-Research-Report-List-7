<p><p>Backing pumps are essential in the semiconductor market, providing vacuum systems that enhance process efficiency. Liquid ring pumps utilize a rotating liquid to create a consistent vacuum, suitable for handling moisture-laden gases. Gas-circulation-cooled pumps use circulating gas for effective cooling, maintaining vacuum levels without overheating. Screw pumps employ helical screws for continuous gas movement, ensuring reliability and high efficiency. Multi-stage roots pumps utilize multiple stages to achieve deep vacuum levels, ideal for demanding semiconductor applications requiring precise control and stability.</p></p>
