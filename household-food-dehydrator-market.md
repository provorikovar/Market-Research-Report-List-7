<p><p>The household food dehydrator market is primarily divided into stackable and movable food dehydrators. Stackable food dehydrators consist of multiple trays that can be expanded vertically, allowing for efficient use of space and easy storage. They are ideal for dehydrating smaller batches of food. Movable food dehydrators, on the other hand, are typically designed as standalone units with a fixed number of trays and more powerful heating elements, offering greater capacity and versatility for larger quantities of produce.</p></p>
