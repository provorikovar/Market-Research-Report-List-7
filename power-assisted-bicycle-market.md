<p><ul><li>Mid-Drive</li><li>Hub-Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power-assisted bicycle market can be divided into two main types: mid-drive and hub-drive systems. Mid-drive bicycles feature motors located at the bike's bottom bracket, providing better weight distribution and efficiency in climbing hills, making them popular among performance-oriented riders. In contrast, hub-drive systems have motors integrated into the wheel hubs, offering a more straightforward design and lower maintenance. Hub drives excel in city commuting due to their simplicity and ease of use, making them a popular choice for casual cyclists.</p></p>
