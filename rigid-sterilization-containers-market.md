<p><p>The rigid sterilization containers market is divided into filter type and valve type systems. Filter type containers utilize high-efficiency filters to maintain airflow while preventing contamination, making them ideal for sterilizing instruments without compromising sterility. Valve type containers, on the other hand, incorporate specialized valves that regulate pressure and airflow, ensuring safe and effective sterilization processes. Both types cater to healthcare settings, providing reliable options for the safe transportation and storage of sterilized medical instruments.</p></p>
