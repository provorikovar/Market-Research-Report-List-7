<p><p>The Cord Blood Stem Cells market is primarily segmented into allogeneic and autologous stem cells. Allogeneic stem cells are harvested from donated cord blood and used for transplantation in genetically different individuals, providing a broader matching pool for patients in need. In contrast, autologous stem cells are derived from an individual's own cord blood, allowing personalized treatment without the risk of rejection. Both types play crucial roles in regenerative medicine and the treatment of various hematological disorders.</p></p>
