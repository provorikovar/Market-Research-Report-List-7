<p><strong>The Direct to Garment Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ink Powder</li><li>Liquid Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct to Garment (DTG) ink market is primarily segmented into two types: ink powder and liquid ink. Ink powder involves a dry formulation that is fused onto fabric using heat, offering durability and vibrant colors. In contrast, liquid ink is a water-based solution that directly saturates the fabric, ensuring a softer feel and intricate designs. Both types cater to different production needs, with liquid ink being favored for detailed prints, while ink powder is preferred for durability and resistance against wear.</p></p>
