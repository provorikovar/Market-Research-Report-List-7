<p><p>The flood detector market is primarily divided into two types: wired and wireless flood detectors. Wired flood detectors are hardwired into a plumbing system or power source, offering reliable and continuous monitoring but requiring installation expertise. In contrast, wireless flood detectors use radio frequencies to transmit alerts, providing flexibility and ease of installation, as they can be placed in various locations without complex wiring. Each type serves distinct user needs based on reliability, setup requirements, and monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
