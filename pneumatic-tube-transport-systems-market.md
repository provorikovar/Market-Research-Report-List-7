<p><p>Pneumatic tube transport systems are categorized into fully automatic and semi-automatic types based on their operational capabilities. Fully automatic systems require minimal human intervention, efficiently transporting materials through a network of tubes using automated controls and sensors. In contrast, semi-automatic systems involve some manual operations, where users may need to load or unload materials, but still benefit from pneumatic technology for transport. Both types are utilized in sectors like healthcare and logistics for efficient material handling and communication.</p></p>
