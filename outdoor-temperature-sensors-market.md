<p><p>The outdoor temperature sensor market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless. Wired outdoor temperature sensors connect directly to a monitoring system, providing reliable data transmission and consistent power supply, making them ideal for permanent installations. Conversely, wireless outdoor temperature sensors offer flexibility and ease of installation, transmitting data via radio frequency or Wi-Fi, which allows for placement in hard-to-reach locations. Both types serve essential roles in various applications, including weather monitoring, HVAC systems, and smart homes.</p></p>
