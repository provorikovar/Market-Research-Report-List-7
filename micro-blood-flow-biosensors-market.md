<p><ul><li>Piezo-electric Sensor Wires</li><li>Pressure Microcatheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro blood flow biosensors market comprises various types, including piezo-electric sensor wires and pressure microcatheters. Piezo-electric sensor wires utilize the piezoelectric effect to convert mechanical stress from blood flow into electrical signals, enabling real-time monitoring of blood dynamics. Pressure microcatheters, on the other hand, are specialized devices that measure pressure within blood vessels, providing critical data for assessing vascular health and guiding clinical interventions. Together, these technologies enhance patient outcomes through precise blood flow analysis and monitoring.</p></p>
