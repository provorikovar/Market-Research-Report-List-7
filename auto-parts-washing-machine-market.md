<p><p>The auto parts washing machine market is segmented into three primary types: ultrasound, spray, and laser. Ultrasound cleaning utilizes high-frequency sound waves to create cavitation bubbles that effectively remove contaminants. Spray cleaning employs high-pressure jets of liquid to dislodge dirt and grease from surfaces. Laser cleaning, on the other hand, uses focused laser beams to vaporize unwanted materials without damaging the underlying parts. Each method offers distinct advantages in efficiency and precision for maintaining automotive components.</p></p>
