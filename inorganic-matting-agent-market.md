<p><p>The inorganic matting agent market is categorized primarily into two types: powder and gel. Powder matting agents are fine particles added to coatings and paints to reduce gloss and improve texture, offering a dry finish. Gel matting agents, on the other hand, provide a smooth, viscous consistency that enhances application and reduces settling in liquid formulations. Both types are widely used in various industries, including automotive, construction, and consumer goods, to achieve desired aesthetic and functional properties.</p></p>
