<p><p>The oil filtration system market is primarily segmented into three types: centrifugal, vacuum, and others. Centrifugal oil filtration utilizes high-speed rotation to separate contaminants from the oil based on density differences, making it efficient for heavy-duty applications. Vacuum oil filtration operates by creating a vacuum to draw oil through filters, effectively removing impurities and moisture. Other types may include magnetic and barrier filtration systems, offering various methods for prolonging oil life and enhancing machinery performance across diverse industries.</p></p>
