<p><p>The radar reflectors market encompasses various types, notably the octahedral corner reflector and the Luneberg lens. The octahedral corner reflector enhances radar signal return through its geometric design, making it effective for navigational aids and safety applications. Conversely, the Luneberg lens provides a more complex, spherical structure that distributes radar signals uniformly, optimizing detection range. Both types are crucial in improving visibility and tracking in maritime, aviation, and surveillance sectors, contributing to enhanced safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
