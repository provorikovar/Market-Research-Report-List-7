Hindered Phenols are widely used in plastics, rubber, and food packaging due to their stability and non-discoloring properties. Phosphites are primarily used as secondary antioxidants, enhancing polymer stability. Thioesters are synergistic with primary antioxidants, improving long-term thermal stability. Other types include blends and specialized antioxidants catering to niche applications across industries like automotive, food, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
