<p><p>The powder compacting press market is categorized into five main types: </p><p>1. **Hydraulic** presses utilize fluid pressure to compact powders, offering high force and flexibility.</p><p>2. **Electric** presses use electric motors for precise control and efficiency, ideal for smaller batch productions. </p><p>3. **Hybrid** models combine hydraulic and electric systems to balance power and control. </p><p>4. **Mechanical** presses function through mechanical systems for speed and robustness, commonly used for high-volume production. </p><p>5. **Servo-hydraulic** presses integrate servo technology for precise control and energy efficiency, enhancing performance in complex applications.</p></p>
