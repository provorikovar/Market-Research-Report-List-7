<p><p>The Epoxy Resin Mold Release Tape market is segmented into two main types: single side and double side. Single side tape features an adhesive on one side, ideal for simple applications where the tape adheres to one surface, providing easy release for molded items. Both types cater to various industries requiring effective mold release solutions.</p></p>
