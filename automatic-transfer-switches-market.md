<p><strong>The Automatic Transfer Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open transition</li><li>Closed transition</li><li>Static transfer switch (STS)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS) market comprises several types, including open transition, closed transition, and static transfer switches (STS). Open transition switches provide a simple and cost-effective solution by disconnecting the load from the utility before connecting to a backup power source. Closed transition switches allow for a brief overlap, enhancing reliability. STS utilize solid-state technology for seamless transfer without interruption, ideal for sensitive applications. Other market variants may include manual transfer switches and bypass isolation switches, addressing diverse user needs.</p></p>
