<p><p>The thermoset glass fiber prepreg market is categorized primarily into epoxy resin, phenolic resin, cyanate ester resin, and others. Epoxy resin is favored for its superior mechanical properties and adhesion, making it ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. Phenolic resin offers excellent thermal resistance and fire properties, commonly used in electrical insulation. Cyanate ester resin combines high thermal stability with low moisture absorption, suitable for high-performance applications. Other resins may include polyimides and urethanes, catering to specialized market needs.</p></p>
