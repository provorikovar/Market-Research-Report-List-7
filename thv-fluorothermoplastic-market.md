<p><p>The THV fluorothermoplastic market encompasses two primary molding techniques: injection molding and extrusion molding. Injection molding involves injecting heated THV material into a mold to create precise, complex shapes, ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, extrusion molding forces THV through a die to produce continuous profiles, sheets, or tubes, suitable for custom applications. Both methods leverage the unique properties of THV, such as chemical resistance and flexibility, catering to diverse industries like automotive, electronics, and medical devices.</p></p>
