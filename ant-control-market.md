<p><p>The ant control market comprises three primary product types: sprays, powders, and baits. Sprays provide a quick, surface application to eliminate visible ants and repel them from treated areas. Powders, suitable for both indoor and outdoor use, create a barrier that ants ingest or transport back to their nests, effectively targeting colonies. Baits, designed to attract ants, contain poison that worker ants carry back, eliminating entire colonies over time. Each type serves distinct needs for consumer pest management.</p></p>
