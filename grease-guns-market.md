Battery-powered grease guns offer portability and ease of use, ideal for frequent applications without manual pumping. Pneumatic grease guns utilize compressed air for dispensing, providing high efficiency and speed, suitable for heavy-duty operations. Each type caters to specific user needs, from casual maintenance to industrial applications, ensuring diverse market demand.</p></p>
