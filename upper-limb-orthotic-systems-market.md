<p><p>The Upper Limb Orthotic Systems market is categorized into soft braces and rigid braces. Soft braces, made from flexible materials, provide support while allowing for movement, making them ideal for conditions requiring stabilization without immobilization. Rigid braces, on the other hand, are constructed from stiffer materials to immobilize the affected area, offering maximum support for severe injuries or post-surgery recovery. Both types cater to diverse patient needs, enhancing rehabilitation and functional recovery in upper limb conditions.</p></p>
