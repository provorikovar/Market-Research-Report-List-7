<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Keyless Vehicle Access Control Systems enable vehicle entry and ignition without traditional keys. **Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES)** automatically unlock doors when the key fob is in proximity, using sensors to detect the user's presence. **Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES)** require manual input, such as pressing a button on the key fob to unlock or lock the vehicle. Both systems enhance convenience and security, with PKES offering hands-free operation and RKES providing controlled access from a distance.</p></p>
