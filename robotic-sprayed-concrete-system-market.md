<p><p>The Robotic Sprayed Concrete System market is categorized into two primary types: dry mix and wet mix. </p><p>Dry mix involves pre-mixing dry cement, aggregates, and additives, which are then combined with water at the nozzle for application. This method provides enhanced control and reduced rebound. </p><p>In contrast, the wet mix system combines materials and water beforehand, delivering a ready-to-use mixture. This approach allows for faster application and better consistency, making it ideal for large-scale projects and challenging environments.</p></p>
