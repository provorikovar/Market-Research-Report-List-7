<p><p>The Organic Virgin Coconut Oil market primarily consists of two types: cold-pressed and expeller-pressed. Cold-pressed oil is extracted at low temperatures, preserving its natural flavor, aroma, and nutrients, making it highly sought after for culinary and cosmetic uses. In contrast, expeller-pressed oil uses a mechanical process applying heat and pressure, which can affect the oil's characteristics but often results in higher yields. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers looking for high-quality coconut oil for various applications.</p></p>
