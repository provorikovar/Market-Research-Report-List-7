<p><strong>The Beverage Centrifugal Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage Pump</li><li>Multi-stage Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The beverage centrifugal pump market encompasses two primary types: single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps, featuring one impeller, are designed for low-pressure applications, efficiently transferring liquids like water or juices in bottling processes. In contrast, multi-stage pumps consist of multiple impellers, enabling them to generate higher pressures, making them ideal for applications requiring significant elevation, such as moving beverages in large production facilities. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring efficient fluid movement within the beverage industry.</p></p>
