<p><p>The ballistic protection fabric market is primarily segmented into two main types: polyethylene-based ballistic protection fabric and aramid-based (aromatic amide) ballistic protection fabric. Polyethylene-based fabrics, known for their high strength-to-weight ratio, provide excellent protection while being lightweight and flexible. Both types are crucial for enhancing safety in various defense and law enforcement environments.</p></p>
