<p><p>Neonicotinoid pesticides are a class of neuro-active insecticides modeled after nicotine, targeting the nervous system of insects. Additionally, there is a heightened focus on integrated pest management (IPM) strategies, which incorporate neonicotinoids as part of broader pest control regimes.
