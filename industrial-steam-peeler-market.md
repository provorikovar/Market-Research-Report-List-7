<p><ul><li>Batch Steam Peeler</li><li>Continuous Steam Peeler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Steam Peeler market consists of two primary types: batch steam peelers and continuous steam peelers. Batch steam peelers process a fixed quantity of products in distinct loads, making them suitable for smaller operations or varying product volumes. In contrast, continuous steam peelers operate non-stop, allowing for high-throughput processing, ideal for large-scale production environments. Both types utilize steam to efficiently remove skins from food products, improving productivity and reducing labor costs in the food processing industry.</p></p>
