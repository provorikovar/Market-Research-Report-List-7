<p><p>Fluorescence immunoassay analyzers are categorized into several market types. Enzyme-linked fluorescent immunoassays utilize enzyme labels to produce a fluorescent signal upon substrate reaction, enabling sensitive detection of analytes. Chemiluminescence immunoassays generate light through chemical reactions, offering high sensitivity with low background noise. Radioimmunoassays use radioactive isotopes for detection, allowing precise quantification but raising safety concerns. Other market types may include newer or hybrid technologies that combine elements from these methods, enhancing performance and broadening application scope in diagnostics.</p></p>
