<p><strong>The Aluminum Alloy Wheels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting</li><li>Forging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum alloy wheels market is primarily segmented into casting and forging types. Casting involves pouring molten aluminum into molds, offering cost-effective production with diverse designs, suitable for mass-market vehicles. Forging, on the other hand, uses high pressure to shape solid aluminum, resulting in stronger, lighter, and more durable wheels, often preferred for high-performance and luxury vehicles. While casting dominates due to affordability, forging is gaining traction for its superior strength-to-weight ratio and premium appeal. Both methods cater to varying consumer needs and automotive applications.</p></p>
