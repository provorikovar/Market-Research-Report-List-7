<p><p>Activated carbon is a highly porous form of carbon processed to have small, low-volume pores that increase its surface area, making it effective for adsorption. Activated carbon is derived from materials like coconut shells, wood, and coal, and its applications span industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, automotive, and environmental protection.</p><p>The Activated Carbon Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.3% during the forecast period.
