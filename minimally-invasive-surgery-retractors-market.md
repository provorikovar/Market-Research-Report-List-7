<p><p>Minimally invasive surgery retractors are specialized instruments used to hold back tissues and create space for surgeons during procedures. The market features different types: 2-way retractors allow for tension in two directions, ideal for small incisions, while 3-way retractors enable tension in three directions, providing enhanced visibility and access. 4-way retractors offer even greater versatility with tension in four directions, accommodating larger surgical areas. Each type is designed to improve surgical efficiency and patient outcomes while minimizing trauma.</p></p>
