RSA algorithm employs asymmetric encryption, enabling secure key exchange and digital signatures. AES algorithm, widely adopted for its speed and robust security, encrypts data in fixed block sizes, making it ideal for protecting large volumes of information. Each algorithm caters to different security needs within the document encryption market.</p></p>
