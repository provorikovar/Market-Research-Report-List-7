<p><p>The Slow and Controlled Release Fertilizers market can be segmented into two main types: Binary Compound Fertilizers and NPK Compound Fertilizers. Binary Compound Fertilizers contain two primary nutrients, typically nitrogen and phosphorus or nitrogen and potassium, delivering essential nutrients more steadily to plants. NPK Compound Fertilizers encompass a balanced blend of nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K), providing a comprehensive nutritional profile for crops. Both types enhance nutrient efficiency, reduce leaching, and promote sustainable agricultural practices.</p></p>
