<p><p>The carbon fiber tape market is primarily divided into two types: prepreg tape and dry tape. Prepreg tape is pre-impregnated with resin, offering enhanced durability and uniformity, ideal for aerospace and automotive applications requiring high strength and stiffness. In contrast, dry tape consists of carbon fibers without resin, allowing for flexibility in manufacturing processes. This type is often used in situations requiring customized composite shapes and is popular in industries like sporting goods and construction due to its lightweight nature.</p></p>
